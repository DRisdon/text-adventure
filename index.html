INTERDIMENTIONAL EVIL WIZARD LAPTOP RESCUE

An EVIL wizard has stolen your laptop and disappeared into another dimension! Go get it back! You will encounter strange people and creatureson your journey, so be prepared for a fight!
At each scene, you will be presented with a series of choices. Each choice you make will affect how the story progresses. Some choices might hurt you, while others might heal you. If you reach the final scene and get you laptop back, YOU WIN!
